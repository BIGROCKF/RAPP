1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address

User Content

  • Images you upload for AI processing
  • Text prompts or other inputs submitted for AI generation

The App does not display third-party advertisements.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide AI-generated results requested by the user

3. AI Processing and Data Transmission

When a user uploads an image or submits text input for AI generation, this content is securely transmitted through our server infrastructure and forwarded to Fal AI (fal.ai) solely for the purpose of accessing artificial intelligence models and generating the AI results requested by the user.

Before any image or text input is transmitted to Fal AI, users are clearly informed within the App and must explicitly consent to this data transmission.

  • Uploaded images and text inputs are used exclusively for AI result generation.
  • We do not use user content for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.
  • We do not sell user data to any third parties.
  • User content is temporarily processed and automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

Fal AI processes the transmitted data solely for providing AI model access and processing services. To the best of our knowledge, Fal AI does not use submitted data for independent marketing purposes.

All payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive, store, or process payment card information or financial data.

We may share user information only in the following circumstances:
